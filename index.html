Glorious Pattern Matcher

In the left box, type a series of characters representing an ordered pattern. In the right box, type a series of words separated by spaces. The ⚙ button will let you know if the words match the pattern specified in the first box.

Examples

AAA

Ho Ho Ho

Match

AAA

Oh No No

No match

ABBA

Knowing Me Knowing You

No match

ABBA

Knowing Me Me Knowing

Match
